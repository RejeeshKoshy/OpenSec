Modern SOCs rely on heavy Deep Learning models for anomaly detection, which are unsuitable for resource-constrained Edge environments. This paper proposes a lightweight hybrid framework. We evaluate the performance trade-offs between statistical profiling (Z-Score) and unsupervised learning (Isolation Forest) on a legacy hardware testbed.
